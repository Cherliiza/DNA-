We are a professional, stylish and modern children's creative studio in Lviv! Why do we talk about us so confidently? DNA dance studio is a new approach to revealing a child's talents, potential and individuality; it is a modern vision of the correct aesthetic tastes and creative abilities of the younger generation. We educate creative, active, bright artists-personalities.

We work with children and adolescents aged 3 to 16 years. We offer training in the following areas: pop vocals, guitar and choreography (which is divided into contemporary, hip-hop, freestyle, K-pop, jazz funk, ballroom dancing, Kids Dance (3-5 years old). Both group and individual classes are possible We have our own convenient and objective system for dividing age and level groups, so every child will feel worthy and confident in his place.

Our specialty is that we work in a group format. Starting from the second year of study, we divide the participants into choreographic or vocal-choreographic projects based on a preview or casting. A director works on each number of the band's DNA. There is a selection of content, choreography, images, video and photo series according to the format of the project, which includes children.

In addition, DNA dance studio is engaged in production, filming, implementation of TV, film, and Internet projects. Therefore, we offer our students participation in professional video projects of their own production and in joint projects with partners and colleagues. DNA dance studio is author's content. Together with our musician partners, we create something completely new and fantastic.

We never stand still. We are constantly developing and improving. We are looking for interesting ideas, new solutions to meet the modern interests of children and keep up with trends! With us, your child will discover new things, gain confidence, develop his talent and definitely find real friends, because DNA dance studio is constant performances, shootings, photo shoots, concerts, competitions, open lessons, trips, festivals, camps. So don't hesitate! Make sure that your child's place is right here. Join our team now!

Groups are divided by levels:

Beginners (First year of dancing):

  •  groups of up to 15 people;
  •  classes twice a week;
  •  lesson duration - 1 hour;
  •  the possibility of individual classes;
  •  one performance is mandatory for the first year of study ;
  •  open control lessons;
  •  direction of group productions; ;
  •  the opportunity to participate in castings for studio projects; 
  •  selection of costumes, partial image creation; 
  •  system of discounts for participants who are engaged in two areas; 
  •  the manager can offer a transition to the mix level during training at the beginner level with rapid assimilation of new knowledge and growth in one's direction; 
  •  after completion of the beginners course, - allocation of participants to studio projects by casting.

Middle (Second/Third year of dancing):

  •  distribution of participants into groups according to the projects in which they are involved; 
  •  groups of up to 10 people; 
  •  classes two or three times a week; 
  •  lesson duration 1 hour - 1.5 hours; 
  •  3-4 performances of various formats per year;
  •  the possibility of individual classes;
  •  open control lessons;
  •  directing group productions;
  •  the opportunity to participate in castings for studio projects; 
  •  the opportunity to participate in castings for larger-scale projects (local, regional, all-Ukrainian level of filming);
  •  opportunity to participate in master classes and seminars;
  •  selection of costumes, partial image creation;
  •  system of discounts for participants who are engaged in two areas at once; 
  •  author's content at will;
  •  work in recording studios as desired.
